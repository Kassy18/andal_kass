About Us

Jollywaves's a waterpark and resort for everyone to have fun

Feel the Wave

Jollywaves is an integrated water and adventure theme park and resort which features one of the biggest wave pools in the country, a treasure trove of slides, zip lines, sky bicycles, pool playground, daytime accommodation (huts and cottages), pool bar, a food park, and a pavilion and events place.
