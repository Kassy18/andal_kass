About Us

Jollywaves's a waterpark and resort for everyone to have fun

Feel the Wave

Jollywaves is an integrated water and adventure theme park and resort which features one of the biggest wave pools in the country, a treasure trove of slides, zip lines, sky bicycles, pool playground, daytime accommodation (huts and cottages), pool bar, a food park, and a pavilion and events place.

Jolly Waves Waterpark & Resort offers a range of attractions suitable for both kids and adults, making it an ideal destination for a family outing. However, it's advisable to check the rules and regulations beforehand to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for all visitors.

Features

Wave Pool: It boasts one of the biggest wave pools in the country.

Slides: The resort offers a treasure trove of slides for guests to enjoy.

Adventure Activities: Visitors can experience zip lines, sky bicycles, and a pool playground.

Accommodation: Daytime accommodation options include huts and cottages.

Facilities: There is a pool bar, food park, pavilion, and events place on the premises.

Operating Hours: Jolly Waves Waterpark & Resort operates from Monday to Sunday, from 09:00 to 17:00.

Feedback

The resort has received mixed reviews from visitors. Some have praised its facilities, cleanliness, and family-friendly environment.

On the other hand, there have been complaints about strict rules regarding attire, additional costs for rides, and restrictions on certain activities for younger guests.
